Online Ambulance Service

Developed a mobile application that connects users with nearby ambulance services in real time, significantly improving emergency response efficiency and saving lives. The app streamlines the process of requesting emergency medical assistance and ensures rapid response through advanced location-based technology. I designed and implemented a user-friendly interface for quick and intuitive ambulance requests, integrated Google Maps API for real-time location tracking and optimized routing, enabled push notifications to instantly alert users and ambulance drivers, implemented secure user authentication and emergency contact management features, and collaborated with backend developers to ensure reliable data flow and system stability.
